Research Experience

  • Centre for Visual Information Technology, IIIT-Hyderabad with Prof. Avinash Sharma

    • Building an auto-encoder based deep learning network to construct a voxelised model of human bodies including texture from RGBD images (1 or more).
    • Goal is to preserve texture and clothing data too.
    • Integrating temporal data in form of optical flow.
    • Using different networks for pose and shape reconstruction.
    • The reconstructed model is parametric and so can be animated by changing the parameters.

    • Working in the field of computer vision.

    • Tried out various devices like Kinect and Leap motion to include a depth channel for better classification.
    • Working to replace faces of strangers in photos with artificial faces hallucinated through a GAN to preserve privacy without using methods that damage the quality of the photograph.

  • Cognitive Science Lab, IIIT-Hyderabad with Prof. Vinoo Alluri
